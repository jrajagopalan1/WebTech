About Ideal Clothing

Ideal Clothing is a place to buy, sell, or trade clothing. We are a local business based out of Sherman Connecticut and we hope to provide
a better way to shop for "one of a kind" items. We started this endeavor because we recognized a common problem in the second hand clothing industry that we
can solve by creating a platform that acts as a middleman. Ideal Clothing serves as a way to save both parties money by cutting out unnecessary additional fees.
